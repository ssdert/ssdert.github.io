INK TIME

Time is not an empirical concept. For neither coexistence nor succession would be perceived by us.

INK TIME is regarded as a model of water clock. Ink reservoir will drop ink in fixed time interval Ink turned into stunning, elegance pattern as soon as it contact the surface of water.

1. INK TIME use sensor to detect ink. Creating a non-stoppable dropping cycle.
2.when liquor getting darker user can check color chips to measure time.

INK TIME could be used as a hourcounter to remind you of relaxing.
